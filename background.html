Our Background

The story of PixovaXR's journey in the world of extended reality

Our Beginning

PixovaXR emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge XR technologies and practical business applications. Our founders, a team of technology innovators and creative visionaries, recognized the transformative potential of extended reality and set out to make these technologies accessible and impactful for organizations across industries.

What began as a small team of passionate XR enthusiasts has grown into a leading XR solutions provider with a global client base and a reputation for excellence in innovation and implementation.

Our Culture

At PixovaXR, we foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. We believe that the best solutions emerge when diverse perspectives come together in an environment that encourages creativity and experimentation.

Our team members are passionate about XR technologies and committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible. We value:

  • Innovation: Continuously exploring new approaches and technologies
  • Collaboration: Working together across disciplines to create integrated solutions
  • Excellence: Maintaining the highest standards in everything we do
  • Impact: Focusing on solutions that deliver meaningful results
  • Learning: Constantly expanding our knowledge and skills

This culture has enabled us to attract top talent and build a team that consistently delivers exceptional XR experiences for our clients.
