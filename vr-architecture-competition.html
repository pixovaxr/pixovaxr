VR Architecture Competition

Revolutionizing architectural design competitions through immersive virtual reality experiences

Project Overview

The VR Architecture Competition platform is a groundbreaking solution that transforms traditional architectural design competitions into immersive virtual reality experiences. Developed by PixovaXR, this innovative platform allows architects, designers, and competition judges to experience architectural designs in full-scale virtual environments before they're built.

Immersive VR Experience

Immersive VR walkthrough of architectural designs with realistic spatial awareness and interactive navigation.

Real-time Visualization

Real-time lighting and material visualization with advanced rendering for accurate design representation.

Collaborative Review

Collaborative review sessions for judging panels with shared virtual spaces and real-time feedback tools.

Interactive Elements

Interactive elements to test spatial functionality with clickable objects and environmental interactions.
