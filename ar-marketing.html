Luxury Retail Brand AR Try-On Case Study
Challenge

A luxury fashion brand was facing challenges with online sales of accessories, as customers were hesitant to purchase high-value items without trying them on. Return rates were high, impacting profitability.

Solution

We developed a mobile AR application that allowed customers to virtually try on the brand's accessories, including glasses, jewelry, and watches. The app featured:

  • Realistic 3D models of the entire product catalog
  • Advanced face and hand tracking for accurate placement
  • Customization options for different materials and colors
  • Seamless integration with the brand's e-commerce platform
  • Social sharing functionality
Results

The AR try-on application delivered impressive results:

  • 35% increase in online sales of accessories
  • 40% reduction in return rates
  • Average session time of 6.5 minutes (3x longer than standard product pages)
  • 25,000+ social media shares in the first month
  • Featured in major fashion and technology publications
